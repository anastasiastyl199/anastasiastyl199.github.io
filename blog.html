SWOT Analysis

Strengths:

My strengths are my determination; when I put my mind on something I don't let go until it is done. I am also very creative and like to think outside the box. Additionally, I consider myself open-minded, respecting every opinion because a person's mindset constantly evolves.

Weaknesses:

My weaknesses are that I might need to see or hear something multiple times for it to stick in my memory. Another weakness is that I like to speak my mind; if I disagree with something, I will openly discuss it.

Opportunities:

I see opportunities everywhere. Opportunities present themselves to those who choose to notice them. Taking opportunities is how an individual moves forward in life; why settle for okay when there is potential for great?

Threats:

To me, a threat is anything you allow to be one. If I choose to be optimistic, my mindset prevents anything from presenting itself as a real threat.
