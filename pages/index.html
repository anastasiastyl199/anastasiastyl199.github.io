HBO-ICT Programme

Why I chose this programme:

I chose this programme because I have always been fascinated by technology, and how it shapes our world. I enjoy working with computers and software, and I wanted to pursue a career that allows me to be innovative and make a positive impact.

I believe the HBO-ICT programme suits me because I enjoy solving problems and thinking logically. Technology constantly challenges me to learn new things and find creative solutions, which keeps me motivated. I like the idea of building projects that can actually make life easier for people and improve the way we work and communicate.

Lastly, I chose this programme because, the HBO-ICT course can provide a mix of useful values like:

  1. Broad technical foundation
  2. Research and innovation skills
  3. Critical thinking
